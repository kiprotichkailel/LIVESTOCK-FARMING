Types of Livestock Farming

Cattle Farming

Raising cows for milk and beef.

Space: 1-2 acres per cow

Feeding: Pasture and grains

Revenue: Milk and beef production

Goat Farming

Goats for meat, milk, and fiber.

Space: 0.5 to 1 acre for 10 goats

Feeding: Hay and supplemental grains

Revenue: Meat, milk, and fiber

Pig Farming

Pigs raised for meat production (pork).

Space: 100-150 sq. feet per pig

Feeding: Grains and vegetable scraps

Revenue: Pork production and piglets
