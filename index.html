Project Overview

Poultry farming involves raising chickens for meat (broilers) or eggs (layers). This project is designed to produce high-quality products while ensuring sustainability and profitability. We plan to start with 100 chickens and scale up as the business grows.

Market Analysis

Target Market

  • Broilers (Meat): Local butcheries, supermarkets, hotels.
  • Layers (Eggs): Households, bakeries, restaurants.

Market Opportunity

The poultry farming market is growing, with high demand for both meat and eggs. By offering a consistent supply of quality products, we aim to establish a strong market presence.

Revenue Projections

For broilers, selling 100 birds at Ksh 400–500 each will generate Ksh 40,000–50,000 per cycle. For layers, with 100 hens producing 250–300 eggs each per year, revenue will range from Ksh 375,000–450,000 annually.
